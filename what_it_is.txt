# AI Cyberdeck

AI Cyberdeck is a local-first AI operating environment designed to act as a personal control plane for knowledge, workflows, automation, and system interaction.

The system is built around three layers:

* Frontend: user interfaces and dashboards.
* Core: orchestration, decision-making, workflows, and agents.
* Services: integrations with models, memory systems, files, terminals, databases, and operating-system capabilities.

The goal is not to build a chatbot.

The goal is to build a platform capable of:

* running local AI models,
* managing personal knowledge,
* executing workflows,
* coordinating agents,
* monitoring system resources,
* and eventually interacting with the local computer through controlled tool execution.

Over time the platform evolves from a simple AI service into a personal AI operating environment where intelligence, automation, and system capabilities are unified behind a single control plane.
